Find out more about me

I am a Speech and Language therapist working both privately and for the NHS.
I graduated from Newcastle University with a first class honours degree in Speech and Language Therapy. I am a member of the Royal College of Speech and Language Therapist and have professional Registration with the HCPC (health care professions council).

    I am experienced in working with Children with Speech Language and Communication needs such as: Speech and Language difficulties, Autism and Social Communication difficulties and Stammering.

My additional qualifications:
- Trained in using the Picture Exchange Communication System (PECS)
- Fully competent Makaton user
- Experience in implementing Palin Parent Child Interaction for children with a Stammer
- Trained in implementing alternative communication methods (AAC)
- Experience in delivering Hanen More than words programme for children with autism or social communication difficulties
- Highly experienced in advicing educational professionals and implementing the SCERTS model.
